Trakstar Sensor Characterization

I characterized and assessed the accuracy of trakstar sensor performance within close proximity of one another. Through my experimentation and data analysis I was able to identify a configuration of trakstar sensors which not only decreased the sensor footprint within the MyHand Robot by 75% and improve joint angle reporting accuracy to within 5 degrees.

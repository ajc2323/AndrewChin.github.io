Compost Smart

Overview

CompostSmart is designed to bring personalized information for your compost bins. Our bin and app empower volunteers by identifying optimal compost materials, monitoring essential metrics for compost health, and creating a network for gardeners to exchange tips on maintaining healthy bins.

Problem Identification

Composting is key for maintaining a sustainable garden, yet busy NYC community gardeners struggle with compost pile optimization. Composting is important but it seems like nobody knows how to do it right. Sure - there are google groups that share information, but they are disorganized and lacks specifics. Evenmore, knowledgeable gardeners do not have the right bin to compost efficiently.

Design & Prototyping

We built a the CompostSmart prototype within Figma to shocase its 3 main features:

  • Compost Material Identification & Education
  • Compost Health Monitoring
  • Community Garden Information and Resource Sharing Network

Impact & Learnings

The prototype helped community gardens monitor compost conditions with ease. Gardeners reported improved compost consistency and reduced odor. Future iterations will explore CO₂ sensing and long-range data logging using LoRa. This project highlighted the value of hardware design for urban sustainability and education.
